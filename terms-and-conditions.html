Terms and Conditions

  1. Project Commencement
    All projects will commence only after advance payment is received and the agreement is signed.
  2. Domain & Hosting Management
    At TenSketch, we handle end-to-end digital services, including domain management, hosting, code maintenance, support, and ongoing maintenance for every client. We either purchase the client's domain or request its transfer to our account for seamless auto-renewals. This enables clients to focus on their business while we manage technical aspects.

    For businesses in critical security categories (e.g., banking, finance, large healthcare, or education organizations), the client reserves the right to retain control of domain and server as part of our special secured client service.

    The same applies to code ownership: only special secured category clients will have the right to own the project code.
  3. Termination & Transfer
    If a client wishes to terminate the agreement before the agreed tenure, they must pay a total of 5 years' maintenance fees plus 5 times the initial development fee. Upon payment, we will transfer the domain to the client; however, the code remains the property of TenSketch. This does not apply to special secured category clients, who retain in-house ownership from the outset.

    Clients cannot request removal of credits unless they pay for or license the asset themselves.

    TenSketch will provide an estimated project timeline. Timelines may extend if delays are caused by incomplete requirements, late approvals, or unresponsive communication from the client.

Ongoing maintenance beyond this period or outside the agreed scope requires a separate maintenance contract or renewal.
