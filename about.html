About Us

The name TenSketch means "Perfect Arts." The number ten (10) symbolizes completeness. In the binary system, '1' and '0' form the foundation of digital calculation. On a rating scale, ten (10) is the highest score, representing perfection. "Sketch" signifies a dedication to the arts and design. We brand ourselves with this meaning and concept.
